GROW Weather

A game based on GROW, a game made by Eyemaze. If you are unfamilar with GROW, you could read my Introduction to GROW PowerPoint below.

How to play GROW?

  1. Click the items in sequence
  2. The items will interact with each other
  3. Observe the results
  4. Improve your sequence
  5. Guess the correct sequence after millions of tries, which results in all items at level max.
